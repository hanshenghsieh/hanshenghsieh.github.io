The Popular Science Exhibition of Math and Art

Mathematics has always been one of the most mysterious and fantastic discoveries of human beings over the centuries. However, when mentioning mathematics, most people only recall the tedious math exercises and stressful exams in high school, and it is often considered as the language that can only be spoken by the super genius alike people. Seldom do we know actually that mathematics isn’t merely about calculating or logics, but also it is capable of doing graphic pictures and create different kinds of visual art works in many ways. Professor Chen Ming Zhang therefore decided to hold this exhibition and introduced the concept of the “Structural Cloning” to the public by showing people the amazing art works that have been done accordingly and explained the theory behind it.

“Open Another Window Between Math and Art”
This is the idea and the tagline raised by Professor Chen. In order to cohere the idea and present the concept of the structural cloning to the audience, the design of the poster brings out the similar style of art work that relates to the structural cloning with the simple rectangle as the geometrical item that initiates the infinitive expansion and represents the idea of the window mentioned in the tagline.
