2016 The Freshmen Guidance

The freshman guidance manual is more than just a pile of important information for the freshiee; it is also an illusion that evokes the imagination of the college life. Being the head of the department of marketing at NCTU Student Union in 2016, I was responsible for the freshman guidance project, leading the department to conduct the design of the manual and the campaign of the guidance for establishing the very first image of the Student Union among the freshman. The guidance manual has incorporated different information from the school, the sponsorships and the cooperative organisation. In order to consist the coherency of the visual design in the guidance manual, we also took charge of the design of the advertisement from the sponsors with the collaboration of the department of public relation. Overall, the guidance manual was designed by every member in the department of marketing who came to the consensus of the style of the guidance manuel after several meetings. The presented pictures on this page are the part I was in charge of.
